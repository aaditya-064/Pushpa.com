Introduction

In my research essay, I explore the education system in Nepal. I am already aware that the education system in Nepal was based on home

schooling and Gurukul, but since they become a democracy in 1951, they have made many improvements. It is my intention to find out what

those improvements are.

Methology

My main methodology will include reading written reasearch on the subject from local libraries, University libraries, and trusted online

resources. I will also research and read records and websites realting to trusted statistics. They have school and higher education, where a student has to apply for

higher education as people have in other countries. A student gets a school-leaving certificate for completing school and getting their education. Grades 11 and 12 are

considered higher secondary education, for which a student gets a certificate for completing by the HSEB, which was the Higher Secondary Education Board.
